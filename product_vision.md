# Product Vision: Forgiving Self-Improvement App

### 1. The Core Problem Statement
Current habit-tracking and addiction-recovery apps rely heavily on streak-based mechanics and loss aversion. This design creates friction and anxiety: when a user relapses, the "streak" resets to zero, inducing FOMO, guilt, and a high probability of total goal abandonment. Furthermore, standard apps demand high daily interaction, leading to tracking fatigue.

### 2. Target Audience & MVP Scope
* **Audience of One:** The MVP is built strictly for the Product Owner to dogfood. It is entirely self-centered to validate the core loop.
* **Out of Scope for MVP:** Market adoption, onboarding flows, user account management, settings menus, monetization (ads/subscriptions), and generalized retention features.

### 3. Core Value Proposition (CVP)
A resilient, low-friction tracking ecosystem that rewards consistency without punishing failure. It replaces the anxiety of "maintaining a streak" with the satisfaction of "nurturing an autonomous entity" (e.g., a garden or pet) backed by tangible, real-world data (health recovery and money saved).

### 4. The Core Engagement Loop
* **The Frictionless Daily Input:** Zero app-opens required. Tracking is handled entirely via rich, interactive daily push notifications (e.g., *"Did you smoke today? [Yes] / [No]"*). 
* **The Deep Weekly Ritual:** The primary app interface is reserved for a deliberate, once-a-week session. This session aggregates the week's data, displays hard statistical progress (money saved, biological milestones), and triggers the evolution of the autonomous entity.
* **The Forgiving Relapse Protocol:** A negative log (relapse) does not kill the entity or reset progress to zero. It triggers a "weathering" or "setback" visual state that pauses growth. Recovery is achieved through honest logging and simple restorative actions during the weekly review.

### 5. Product Success Metrics (Phase 1)
Since traditional DAU/WAU metrics are irrelevant for an audience of one, success is defined by two personal behavioral KPIs:
* **Friction Tolerance:** The ability to answer the daily push notification consistently for weeks without feeling tracking fatigue.
* **Behavioral Impact:** The weekly review sessions and the autonomous entity's visual state successfully deter relapse and replace the dopamine hit previously provided by streak-counting.
